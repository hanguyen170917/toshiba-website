    Acrylic Resin Coated coil can reduce the formation of a drop of water or oil on the coil unit also the dust is hardly to stick on coil.

  • Swing & Fix Louver

    Use this to select the desired air direction or swing operation.

  • ECO

    Achieve energy-savings of up to 25% compared with standard without sacrificing comfort

  • Fan Speed

    5 Fan Speed with comfortable setting, puls Auto Fan and Hi-Power modes. You can choose from gentle airflow, right up to the full cooling of Hi-Power mode.

  • Hi-Power

    Hi-Power mode made your room cool faster yet quiet when operating. When you come home on the hot day, just press on the “Hi-Power” button. Toshib’s extra airflow rapidly delivers extra cooling throughout the room without making undesired noise.
